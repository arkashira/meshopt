# Customer Journey Map for MeshOpt

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developers encounter performance issues or complexity in managing service meshes during application deployment.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about existing solutions; confusion over service mesh concepts and their benefits.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration due to inefficiencies; curiosity about potential solutions.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blogs, webinars) that demystifies service meshes; targeted ads highlighting pain points and solutions.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content engagement rates.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developers actively search for service mesh management solutions after recognizing their current challenges.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the variety of tools available; difficulty in comparing features and benefits.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism about claims made by various products; hope for finding a suitable solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison guides; case studies showcasing successful implementations; free trials or demos.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and downloads of comparison guides.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developers sign up for a trial or demo of MeshOpt.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity in initial setup; lack of clear onboarding instructions; potential integration issues with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement about trying a new tool; anxiety during setup; frustration if initial hurdles are encountered.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding process; interactive tutorials; responsive customer support during trial.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developers successfully integrate MeshOpt into their workflow and see improvements.
- **Friction Points**: Resistance to change from existing tools; potential performance issues during full implementation.
- **User Emotions**: Relief from solving previous pain points; satisfaction with improved performance; cautious optimism about long-term usage.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular check-ins from customer success teams; personalized tips for maximizing the platform's benefits.
- **Metric**: User retention rate and frequency of feature usage.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Organizations recognize the value of MeshOpt and consider expanding its use across teams or projects.
- **Friction Points**: Budget constraints; concerns about scaling; potential integration with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the product's value; excitement about broader implementation; apprehension about costs and resources.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Volume discounts for larger teams; success stories from other organizations; additional training resources for teams.
- **Metric**: Number of new licenses purchased and expansion of usage across departments.
