# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for MeshOpt

### Target Integrations

1. **HashiCorp Consul**
   - **Rationale**: Widely used for service discovery and configuration, integrating with Consul will enhance service mesh capabilities and provide seamless configuration management.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for up to 5 nodes.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies service discovery and configuration management for developers.

2. **Istio**
   - **Rationale**: As a leading service mesh technology, integrating with Istio will allow users to optimize and manage their Istio configurations more effectively.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source; no direct costs.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides enhanced monitoring and troubleshooting capabilities for Istio users.

3. **Kubernetes**
   - **Rationale**: Most service meshes operate within Kubernetes environments. Integration will streamline deployment and management of service meshes in Kubernetes clusters.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source; no direct costs.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies deployment and scaling of service mesh applications.

4. **AWS App Mesh**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with AWS App Mesh will attract AWS users looking for optimized service mesh management within their cloud environment.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for up to 1 million requests per month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides AWS users with enhanced visibility and control over their microservices.

5. **DataDog**
   - **Rationale**: DataDog provides monitoring and analytics for cloud applications. Integration will enhance observability for service mesh users.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for up to 5 hosts.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users monitor service performance and troubleshoot issues effectively.

6. **Prometheus**
   - **Rationale**: As a popular monitoring solution, integrating with Prometheus will allow users to leverage metrics for optimizing their service mesh performance.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source; no direct costs.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Enables users to collect and analyze metrics for better performance tuning.

7. **Grafana**
   - **Rationale**: Grafana provides visualization for metrics collected from various sources. Integration will enhance reporting and visualization capabilities for users.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for up to 3 users.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Improves user experience by providing insightful dashboards for service mesh performance.

8. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Twilio can enable real-time notifications and alerts for service mesh events, enhancing operational responsiveness.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free trial with $15 credit.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates immediate communication for critical service mesh events, improving incident response times.

### Summary
The integration roadmap focuses on partnerships that enhance the functionality of MeshOpt while addressing specific user pain points in service mesh management. Prioritizing integrations with affiliate or revenue-sharing opportunities will further strengthen the business model and create additional revenue streams.
